The LAnguages, Machines & Brains Lab is directed by Dr. Jixing Li. Our lab's research intersects the multidisciplinary fields of Linguistics, Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Cognitive Neuroscience. We are always looking for motivated graduate and undergraduate students, postdocs, and visiting scholars to join our team. If interested, please email jixingli@cityu.edu.hk.

Neuroimaging Methods Workshop

This 2-day workshop covers various topics in analysing neuroimaging data, including fMRI, MEG, EEG and ECoG. The goal of the workshop is to introduce neuroimaging concepts and practice analysis skills with hands-on tutorials. Find the speakers details and tutorial titles here.
